Privacy Policy

Introduction

The Daily Grind Coffee Co. ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment details when you create an account, place an order, or contact us. We also collect usage data such as pages visited and browser type.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process orders, manage your rewards account, send relevant communications (with your consent), improve our services, and comply with legal obligations.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted service providers who assist in operating our website, processing payments, or delivering orders — only to the extent necessary to perform those services.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You may also opt out of marketing communications. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@thedailygrind.com.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please reach out to us at privacy@thedailygrind.com or write to: The Daily Grind Coffee Co., 123 Main Street, Brooklyn, NY 11201.
