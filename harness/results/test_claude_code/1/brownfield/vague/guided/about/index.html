Our Story

How It Started

The Daily Grind was born in 2018 in a tiny Brooklyn garage. Founders Maria Chen and David Okafor shared a passion for exceptional coffee and a belief that every neighborhood deserves a welcoming place to slow down and savor a great cup.

What began as weekend pop-ups at local farmers' markets quickly grew into a loyal following. By 2020, we opened our first permanent location on Main Street in Brooklyn — and the rest is history.

Today we operate six locations across New York and New Jersey, but our commitment to quality, community, and sustainability remains exactly the same.

What We Stand For

Ethically Sourced

We partner directly with farms in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala, paying above fair-trade prices.

Locally Roasted

Every batch is roasted in small lots at our Brooklyn roastery, ensuring peak freshness.

Zero Waste Goal

We're working toward zero waste by 2027 through compostable cups, bulk ingredients, and community composting.
