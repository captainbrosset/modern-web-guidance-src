Dark Roast Espresso

Bold flavor, expertly crafted

About This Roast

Our dark roast espresso is made from a blend of Colombian and Guatemalan beans, roasted to a deep, rich profile that brings out notes of dark chocolate, toasted walnut, and a hint of smokiness.

Pulled as a single or double shot on our La Marzocca machines, this espresso forms the backbone of many of our drinks - or stands on its own for purists.
