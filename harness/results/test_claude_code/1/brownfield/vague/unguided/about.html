Our Story

From bean to cup, with love

How It All Started

The Daily Grind was founded in 2018 by two coffee-obsessed friends who believed their city deserved better coffee. What started as a tiny cart at the local farmers market has grown into three beloved locations across town.

We source our beans directly from small farms in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala, paying above fair-trade prices to support sustainable agriculture. Every batch is roasted locally in small quantities to ensure peak freshness and flavor.

Our mission is simple: serve exceptional coffee, support our farmers, and create a warm community space where everyone feels welcome.

Our Values

Ethical Sourcing

Direct relationships with farmers, above fair-trade prices, and full supply chain transparency.

Local Roasting

Small-batch roasting in our downtown facility ensures every cup is at its freshest.

Community First

We host local events, support neighborhood nonprofits, and hire from within the community.
