Our Story

Ember & Brew started in 2018 as a tiny corner roastery with a simple mission: serve honest coffee. We source directly from farmers in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, paying fair prices for the best lots each season.

Every cup is roasted in-house and brewed to order. No shortcuts, no compromises — just great coffee and a warm place to enjoy it.
