Our Story

More than just coffee

Born in a tiny roastery in Portland, Ember & Brew started with a simple idea: coffee should be an experience, not just a drink. We travel to origin farms, build lasting relationships with growers, and roast every batch in-house. From the first sip to the last drop, you'll taste the difference that genuine care makes.
