Our Story

The Ember Cup was born from a simple belief: great coffee brings people together. We source our beans directly from family farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, roasting them in small batches right here in our shop. Every cup is a tribute to the farmers, the craft, and the community we serve.
