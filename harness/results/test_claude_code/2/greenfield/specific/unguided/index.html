Our Story

Founded in 2019, The Roasted Bean started as a weekend pop-up at the local farmers market. Today we roast every batch in-house, source directly from farmers in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala, and serve our community with the same care we put into every cup.

We believe great coffee doesn't need to be complicated — just honest ingredients, careful technique, and a warm place to enjoy it.
