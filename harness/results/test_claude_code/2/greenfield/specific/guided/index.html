Our Story

Ember & Bloom was born from a simple belief: great coffee starts with great relationships—with the farmers who grow the beans, the roasters who coax out every nuance, and the community that gathers around every cup.

We source our beans directly from cooperatives in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Brazil, paying above fair-trade prices to support sustainable farming. Every batch is roasted on-site in our vintage 12-kilo Probat drum roaster.

Whether you're settling in for a long morning or grabbing a quick cup on the way to work, we're glad you're here.
