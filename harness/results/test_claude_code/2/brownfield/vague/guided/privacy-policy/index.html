Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2026

Information We Collect

When you create an account, place an order, or join our rewards program, we may collect:

  • Name and contact information
  • Payment details (processed securely via our payment provider)
  • Order history and preferences
  • Location data (only when you opt in for store-finding features)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Manage your rewards account
  • Send order confirmations and updates
  • Improve our menu and services
  • Send promotional offers (with your consent)

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers who help us operate (payment processors, delivery partners) and only as needed to serve you.

Your Rights

You can request to view, update, or delete your personal data at any time by contacting us at hello@dailygrind.com.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out to us at our contact page or email privacy@dailygrind.com.
