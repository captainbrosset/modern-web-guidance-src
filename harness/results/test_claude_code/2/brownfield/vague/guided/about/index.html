Our Story

From a single cart to your favorite daily ritual.

How It Started

In 2012, founder Maya Chen started The Daily Grind from a converted espresso cart parked outside a downtown bookshop. Armed with a passion for ethically sourced beans and a talent for latte art, she built a loyal following one cup at a time.

What began as a one-person operation has grown into three beloved locations, but the mission remains the same: serve exceptional coffee while doing right by the farmers, the community, and the planet.

What We Believe

Ethical Sourcing

Direct trade relationships with farms in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala.

Local Roasting

Every bean roasted in small batches at our downtown roastery.

Community First

10% of profits support local education and arts programs.
