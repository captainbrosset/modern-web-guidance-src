Our Story

From a single cart to your favorite neighborhood spot.

How It All Started

The Daily Grind began in 2018 as a small coffee cart outside a farmers' market. Founded by two friends with a passion for ethically sourced beans and community, we quickly outgrew our cart and opened our first brick-and-mortar shop downtown.

Today, we operate four locations and roast all our coffee in-house. Every bag of beans is traceable to the farm it came from, and we pay above fair-trade prices to our partners in Guatemala, Ethiopia, Colombia, and Kenya.

We believe great coffee brings people together. That's why our shops are designed as gathering spaces — places to work, meet friends, or simply slow down for a moment.
