Our Story

From a single cart to four Portland locations

How It Started

The Daily Grind began in 2018 as a single coffee cart on the corner of SW 3rd and Burnside in Portland, Oregon. Founders Maya and Carlos Chen had a simple mission: serve the best cup of coffee in the city using ethically sourced beans roasted in small batches right here in Portland.

What We Believe

We believe great coffee starts at the source. We work directly with farmers in Guatemala, Colombia, Ethiopia, and Indonesia, paying above fair-trade prices. Every bag is roasted in our Portland roastery within days of reaching your cup.

Sustainability

All of our cups and packaging are compostable. We partner with local composting programs and donate spent grounds to community gardens. Our goal is zero waste by 2027.

Community

We host weekly cupping sessions, latte art workshops, and open mic nights at our Alberta Arts location. The Daily Grind is more than a coffee shop; it's a gathering place.
