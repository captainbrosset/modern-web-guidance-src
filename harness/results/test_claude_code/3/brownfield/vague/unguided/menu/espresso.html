Dark Roast Espresso

About Our Dark Roast

Our signature dark roast is sourced from small farms in Guatemala and Colombia. Each batch is roasted in-house to bring out deep, smokey notes with hints of dark chocolate and toasted walnut.

Tasting Notes

  • Flavor Profile: Dark chocolate, toasted walnut, smoky caramel
  • Roast Level: Dark (French roast)
  • Origin: Guatemala & Colombia blend
  • Process: Washed
  • Best Served: Straight espresso or Americano
