Our Story

From a single cart to your favorite neighborhood cafe.

How It Started

The Daily Grind began in 2015 as a single coffee cart parked outside the downtown farmers' market. Founded by siblings Maria and Carlos Rivera, the cart quickly gained a loyal following for its ethically sourced, locally roasted beans and genuine hospitality.

Our Mission

We believe great coffee can change your day. Every bean we source is traceable to the farm it came from. We partner with growers in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala who share our commitment to sustainable farming practices and fair wages.

Where We Are Today

With four locations and a growing online presence, The Daily Grind continues to put community first. We roast all our beans locally, compost our grounds, and donate 2% of every sale to local food banks.
