Our Story

Ember & Brew started as a simple idea: what if every cup of coffee could feel like a conversation with the farmer who grew it?

We source our beans directly from smallholder farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala. Each batch is roasted in-house, in small quantities, to bring out the unique character of every origin.

Come find us at 42 Roaster Lane, Downtown. We're open daily from 7 AM to 7 PM.
