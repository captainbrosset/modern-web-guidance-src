Our Story

Founded in 2018, The Daily Grind started as a small cart at the local farmers' market. Today, we bring the same passion and care to our brick-and-mortar shop in downtown.

Every cup begins with ethically sourced beans from small farms across Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala. We roast in small batches every morning so you get the freshest brew possible.
