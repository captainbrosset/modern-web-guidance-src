Our Story

Founded in 2023, Ember & Bloom started as a tiny roastery with one mission: serve coffee that tells a story. We source our beans directly from farmers in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala, paying above fair-trade prices. Every batch is roasted by hand in our open kitchen so you can watch the magic happen.
