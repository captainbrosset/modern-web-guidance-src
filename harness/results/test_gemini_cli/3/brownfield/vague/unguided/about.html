Our Story

Founded in 2018, The Daily Grind started with a simple mission: to bring the highest quality, ethically sourced coffee to our local community.

We believe that coffee is more than just a drink; it's a ritual, a moment of peace in a busy day, and a way to connect with others. That's why we work directly with farmers to ensure fair wages and sustainable practices.

Every bean is roasted in small batches at our local facility to ensure maximum freshness and flavor. From our family to yours, we hope you enjoy every cup.
