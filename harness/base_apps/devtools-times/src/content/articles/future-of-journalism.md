The digital age has democratized information, but it has also unleashed a torrent of misinformation that threatens the foundations of journalism. At the annual Global Media Summit, industry leaders grappled with the challenges of a "post-truth" era.

New AI-powered verification tools were a key topic, promising to help journalists debunk deepfakes and doctored images in real-time. However, speakers also emphasized the irreplaceable value of traditional investigative work and source verification.

Ultimately, the consensus was that the future of journalism depends on a renewed commitment to ethical standards, transparency, and a willingness to engage directly with audiences to rebuild trust.
