The energy was electric as thousands of music lovers gathered for the return of the Summer Echo indie festival. The event, known for its eclectic lineups and vibrant atmosphere, had been sorely missed during its pandemic-induced break.

This year's festival did not disappoint, featuring a mix of established indie darlings and exciting newcomers. From shoegaze to synth-pop, the diverse sounds captivated the audience, who danced and sang along under the summer sky.

The successful return of Summer Echo is a testament to the enduring power of live music and the resilience of the festival community. It was a weekend of pure joy and a sign that the music scene is back, louder and more alive than ever.
