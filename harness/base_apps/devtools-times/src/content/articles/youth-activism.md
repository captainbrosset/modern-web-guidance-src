They are organized, they are passionate, and they are demanding to be heard. A groundswell of youth activism is reshaping the political landscape, bringing a new sense of urgency to long-standing debates.

Unlike previous generations, these young activists are digital natives, masterfully using social media to mobilize support, organize protests, and hold leaders accountable. Their platforms are diverse, but they are united by a common belief that the systems of today are failing to protect the world of tomorrow.

From school strikes for climate to massive online campaigns for social justice, their impact is undeniable. They are proving that you are never too young to make a difference.
