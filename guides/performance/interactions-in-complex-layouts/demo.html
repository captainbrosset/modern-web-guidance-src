Complex Interactions: The Drag & Drop Kanban

This board contains thousands of heavy DOM nodes spread across several columns. Normally, dragging a card out of one column and into another forces the browser to re-calculate the Layout tree for the entire page because heights and positions have changed. By applying content-visibility: auto to the .list columns and the .card elements browser reflows are strictly contained and localized during the drag interaction, massively saving main thread calculation time and ensuring 60FPS animations.
