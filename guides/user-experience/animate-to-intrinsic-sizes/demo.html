Animate To Intrinsic Sizes

By setting interpolate-size: allow-keywords on the :root, you can smoothly animate properties like width and height to and from intrinsic keywords such as auto and max-content using pure CSS.

💡 What is Intrinsic Size? An element's intrinsic size is its natural dimension based entirely on the content it holds, without any explicit fixed lengths applied. It is represented in CSS by sizing keywords like auto, min-content, max-content, and fit-content.
